# How to Send Group Texts to Members Without Email

## For 10 People - Use Your Phone (FREE!)

### iPhone Instructions

1. **Get the phone numbers:**
   - Go to https://cvawvuaa.org/text-notifications.html?admin=1
   - Click "Export Phone Numbers" 
   - OR just write them down from the list

2. **Create a new message:**
   - Open Messages app
   - Tap the compose button (pencil icon)

3. **Add all contacts:**
   - In the "To:" field, type each phone number
   - OR paste them all at once (separated by commas)
   - Example: `5551234567, 5559876543, 5555555555`

4. **Type your message:**
   ```
   CVCWVUAA: Game Watch TOMORROW vs Colorado at NOON! 
   Kickback Jack's, 10330 Midlothian Tpk. 
   Free pizza & wings! 
   Reply STOP if you don't want these updates.
   ```

5. **Send!** That's it. Completely free.

**Note:** Recipients will see each other's numbers and replies go to everyone. For 10 people, this is fine.

---

### Android Instructions

1. **Get the phone numbers** (same as above)

2. **Open Messages app**

3. **Create new message:**
   - Tap the compose button (+)

4. **Add contacts:**
   - Type each phone number in the "To" field
   - OR paste all at once

5. **Type message and send**

## Alternative: Text Them Individually (5 minutes)

If you don't want everyone seeing each other's numbers:

1. Copy your message once
2. Send individual text to each person
3. Takes ~30 seconds per person = 5 minutes total

**Message template:**
```
Hey [NAME], it's Tim from CVCWVUAA! 

Game Watch THIS Saturday vs Colorado at NOON at Kickback Jack's (Midlothian Tpk). 

Chapter is providing FREE pizza & wings! 

Can you make it? 

- Tim
CVCWVUAA President
```
