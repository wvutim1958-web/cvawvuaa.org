üèûÔ∏è OUTDOOR ADVENTURES

Joshua Tree National Park

Distance from Airbnb: 25 miles, 30 minutes
Cost: $30 per vehicle (7-day pass)
Time needed: Half day to full day

Must-See Spots:

  • Keys View - Panoramic desert vista, highest point
  • Skull Rock - Easy 1.7-mile walk, perfect photos
  • Cholla Cactus Garden - 0.25-mile loop, stunning at golden hour
  • Hidden Valley - 1-mile loop trail through rock formations
  • Barker Dam - 1.3-mile loop, usually has water

Tips: Go early morning (cooler!), bring LOTS of water, sunscreen, hats. Pack lunch - limited food options inside.

Salvation Mountain & Salton Sea

Distance from Airbnb: 60 miles, 1 hour
Cost: FREE! (donations appreciated)
Time needed: Half day

The Route:

  • Salvation Mountain - Folk art masterpiece, "God is Love" painted on mountain, bring camera!
  • Bombay Beach - Abandoned town on Salton Sea, post-apocalyptic art installations
  • Salton Sea - California's largest lake, unique ecosystem

Tips: Weird California at its best! Go in afternoon, stay for sunset. Bring picnic lunch. Gets hot - water essential!

Indian Canyons

Distance from Airbnb: 16 miles, 20 minutes
Cost: $12/adult, $6/child
Time needed: 2-4 hours

Three Canyons to Explore:

  • Palm Canyon - 3-mile loop, most popular, palm oasis
  • Andreas Canyon - 1-mile loop, rock formations, stream
  • Murray Canyon - 4 miles round trip, waterfall (seasonal)

Tips: Easy hiking, great for kids! Go morning for cooler temps. Trading post has snacks. Good for granddaughter!

Distance from Airbnb: 20 miles, 25 minutes (Palm Desert)
Cost: $25/adult, $17/child (3-12)

Animals: Giraffes, zebras, cheetahs, bighorn sheep, desert tortoises, meerkats!

Palm Springs Aerial Tramway

Cost: $30/adult, $20/child (3-10)

Tips: Bring jackets! Can be 80¬∞ at bottom, 30¬∞ at top! Last tram down is 9:45 PM. Buy tickets online to save time.
