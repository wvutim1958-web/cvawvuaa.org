💡 Networking Tips

🤝 Making Connections

  • Personalize your messages with shared WVU experiences
  • Mention specific interests or career paths
  • Offer to help before asking for favors
  • Follow up after meeting at events

💼 Professional Networking

  • Connect on LinkedIn after initial contact
  • Schedule coffee meetings for local connections
  • Ask for informational interviews, not jobs
  • Share relevant opportunities with your network
