City of Bridgeport Employee Handbook

Changes Summary: 2023 to 2025

Document Date: October 8, 2025

Executive Summary

This document outlines the major changes, additions, and modifications made to the Employee Handbook between 2023 and 2025. The updates reflect modern workplace practices, enhanced digital considerations, and improved policy clarity.

Major Additions

  • NEW Digital Accessibility Section (3.4)
    • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA compliance requirements
    • Document accessibility standards
    • Alternative format provisions
    • Technology accessibility requirements
  • NEW Alternative Dispute Resolution (7.3)
    • Voluntary mediation program
    • Informal resolution options
    • Structured negotiations process
  • NEW Comprehensive Emergency Response Procedures
    • Detailed protocols for various emergency types
    • Emergency communications procedures
    • Post-emergency procedures
    • Prevention and preparation guidelines

Major Modifications

  • UPDATED Technology Use and Privacy Policies
    • Enhanced data protection measures
    • Remote work technology guidelines
    • Updated security protocols
    • Mobile device management policies
  • UPDATED Special Leave Categories
    • Expanded bereavement leave coverage
    • Enhanced military leave benefits
    • Updated jury duty procedures
    • Revised leave without pay provisions
  • UPDATED Handbook Acknowledgment Forms
    • New specific policy acknowledgments
    • Annual re-acknowledgment requirement
    • Digital signature provisions
    • Policy-specific initialing requirements

Section Restructuring

  • REORGANIZED Policy Organization
    • Section 3 expanded to include Digital Accessibility (3.4)
    • Section 7 reorganized to accommodate ADR (7.3)
    • Emergency procedures consolidated and enhanced

Policy Enhancements

  • ENHANCED Documentation Requirements
    • More detailed documentation procedures
    • Clear timelines for submissions
    • Specific form requirements
  • ENHANCED Compliance Measures
    • Updated legal compliance requirements
    • Enhanced record-keeping procedures
    • Clearer enforcement guidelines

Format and Accessibility Improvements

  • IMPROVED Document Structure
    • Enhanced HTML formatting for better accessibility
    • Improved section navigation
    • Added descriptive headings and subheadings
    • Consistent styling throughout document
