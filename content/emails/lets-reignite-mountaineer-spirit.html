Help Shape the Future of Our Chapter

We need volunteers for Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer. It's one Zoom meeting a month and occasional help with activities.

Email cvcwvuaa@gmail.com if you can help or have ideas: Top Golf, bowling nights, BBQs, fishing tournaments on the James/Lake Chesdin/the Chickahominy, Busch Gardens, Dominion Raceway, a spring golf tourney, and WVU-connected guest speakers.

Annual Meeting & Christmas Plans

Holiday party turnout has been low in recent years. Our bylaws require an Annual Membership Meeting; unless volunteers step forward to plan a Christmas gathering, we'll host the meeting via Zoom to stay compliant.

We may also need to update bylaws to allow a smaller Board. We're working to bring in WVU-connected guest speakers — including a Richmond-area Judge on the WVU Foundation Board and former Mountaineer athletes — for future events.

Gift Card Raffle & A Challenge

We have one $50 and two $25 gift cards ready to raffle at an upcoming Game Watch — and we need a crowd to give them away.

At our last UCF watch we had a small but spirited group; later 50–60 Ole Miss fans packed the same room. If they can do it, we can too. Let's change that starting tomorrow. Bring your Mountaineer pride — and a friend — and help reignite the fire in Central Virginia.
