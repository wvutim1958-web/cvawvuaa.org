Blake E. Fought Memorial Scholarship

The Blake E. Fought Memorial Scholarship is a fund established by the West Virginia University (WVU) Central Virginia Alumni Chapter to honor Blake Fought, a member of the Class of 2008. The scholarship provides funds for non‑resident undergraduate students at WVU, with awards based on both academic achievement and financial need. The scholarship is awarded through the WVU Foundation and the WVU Office of Financial Aid.

  • Donor: Established by friends and family of the Fought family through the CVCM community.
  • Eligibility: Non‑resident undergraduate students enrolled at WVU.
  • Selection Criteria: Academic achievement and financial need.
  • Awarding Body: WVU Foundation and WVU Office of Financial Aid are responsible for determining recipients.
  • Award Structure: Two or more scholarships of at least $500 are awarded annually, with the number and size of awards increasing as the endowment grows.
  • Award Duration: Recipients are eligible for renewal in subsequent years but are limited to a maximum of two consecutive award cycles.

How to Apply / Learn More: For information on applying for the scholarship, students should contact the WVU Office of Financial Aid or the WVU Foundation. You can also read more on the WVU Foundation campaign page or speak with the chapter directly.
