Bears & Blankets

Each year, more than 9,000 children are admitted to WVU Medicine Children’s Hospital, many facing serious health challenges in unfamiliar surroundings. The Bears & Blankets initiative provides comfort through donations of new stuffed animals and blankets.

Beginning in June 2025, Bears & Blankets becomes a national alumni service project. Alumni can donate items locally or give financially via WVU Foundation pages. Donations are given on admission or as treatment rewards to brighten each child’s experience.

Parents and families in Central Virginia can connect with local events and resources through our chapter.
