About the Chapter

Central Virginia Chapter membership is open to all West Virginia University alumni, family, and friends. You do not have to be an alumnus of WVU to join! Your local chapter is a strong network of alumni dedicated to supporting WVU and providing opportunities for fellowship with Mountaineers in nearby Central Virginia. The chapter offers various ways to show your alma mater spirit in the community through social events, Mountaineer athletics, community fundraising and scholarships for new and existing students.

Mission

To foster fellowship, promote Mountaineer spirit, and support the West Virginia University Foundation in its mission to enrich lives and maximize charitable support, both through community fundraising and by providing scholarships for local students attending WVU.

Activities

The WVU Central Virginia chapter fosters professional and social connections for Mountaineers in our region through career networking events, community service projects, and opportunities to engage with current students, all while upholding the WVU values of service, curiosity, accountability, appreciation, and respect.
