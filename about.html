Central Virginia Chapter membership is open to all West Virginia University alumni, family, and friends. You do not have to be an alumnus of WVU to join! Your local chapter is a strong network of alumni dedicated to supporting WVU and providing opportunities for fellowship with Mountaineers in nearby Central Virginia. The chapter offers various ways to show your alma mater spirit in the community through social events, Mountaineer athletics, community fundraising and scholarships for new and existing students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to strengthen connections and collaboration among alumni, students, parents, and the WVU institution, fostering a vibrant community that offers guidance, mentorship, and resources. We are dedicated to empowering students by establishing and growing our scholarship fund for students in our region.
